# EV Design Guide
This document describes the process to design your own EV. We will start with a electric bicycle

## Components of design
- Motor
- Controller
- Battery
- Gears

## Design your own bike
Parameters to capture
- Weight of bike
- weight of rider
- weight of other payloads
- average speed of travel
- max speed of travel
- max gradient of incline
- range

Auto calculated paramters
- weight of motors and batteries
- motor torque and rpm
- gear ratio
- motor power rating (kw)
- battery capacity
- controller power rating
